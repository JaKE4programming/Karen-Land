Karen Land (Hotels)

Here are some hotels in Karen State, particularly around Hpa-An:


  1. Hpa-An Hotel: A budget-friendly option with basic amenities and a convenient location.

  2. Green Hill Hotel: Offers comfortable rooms and is known for its friendly staff and good service.

  3. Zwegabin Taung Resort: Located near the Zwegabin Taung Pagoda, this resort offers beautiful views and a peaceful environment.

  4. Kaw Ka Thaung Hotel: Situated near the famous Kaw Ka Thaung Cave, this hotel provides easy access to one of the top attractions in the area.

  5. Thanlwin Guest House: A cozy guest house with a homely atmosphere, perfect for travelers looking for a more intimate stay.
