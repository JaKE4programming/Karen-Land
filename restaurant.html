Karen Land (Restaurants)

Here are some popular restaurants in Karen State, especially around Hpa-An:


  1. Thai Village Restaurant: Located a bit outside of town on the way to Kawgun Cave, this restaurant offers delicious Thai food with a local twist. They also have vegetarian options on their menu.

  2. Veranda Youth Community Café: If you're looking to relax with a cup of coffee in a garden setting, this café is perfect. It's located on Zwekabin Street and offers a great atmosphere for unwinding.

  3. Two Coins Restaurant: Known for its lovely decoration and fresh atmosphere, this restaurant offers delicious food and drinks. It's a great place to spend an evening in Hpa-An.

  4. San Man Tau: This Burmese restaurant is famous for its variety of small dishes, similar to a King's dining table. It's located on Bo Gyoke Street.

  5. Hpa-An Paradise Bar and Restaurant: The first bar in Hpa-An, this place offers grilled food, Chinese dishes, Thai food, and cocktails. It's a great spot for nightlife in Hpa-An.

  6. Hpa-An Night Market: Located in front of Kan Thar Yar Lake, this night market offers a variety of local foods and drinks. It's a great place to experience the local cuisine with a view of Zwekabin Mountain.
